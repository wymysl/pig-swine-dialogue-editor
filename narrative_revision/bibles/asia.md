# Bible — Asia

**Character:** Asia, front-desk secretary at Pig & Swine
**Role:** office anchor, hint NPC, the firm's actual operational backbone
**Date:** 2026-05-05
**Phase:** 3 (per-character bibles)

---

## Preamble — the contradiction she carries

**Pinned phase-3 question:** Asia is **consciously aware** of the firm's contradictions. She is not naïve about Mr. Pig's framing pattern; she does not buy the wage-suppression performance as descriptive of reality; she sees the structure. She has been at Pig & Swine for years and front-desk staff, in any workplace where the front desk is the actual operational spine, see more than the lawyers do. Asia sees.

She is not a victim of what she sees. She has chosen to stay. The reasons are practical: she likes the work and the people, the alternative job market for someone with her skills is more constrained than it should be, and she has carved out a functional autonomy through operational indispensability that the lawyers do not have. She is not heroic about this. She is not bitter about it. It is her job. She does it well.

She is also still being structurally underpaid — having leverage does not mean the firm is paying her fairly. The two facts coexist. She has the password to the photocopier and the firm has not given her a raise in three years. Both true.

The contradiction she carries is between *seeing everything* and *participating warmly anyway*. She does not editorialize on the case (style_canon §2 rule). She greets people warmly. She asks after their families. She remembers the blue file. She also knows what's in the blue file in ways that go beyond her formal role, and she chooses not to make that knowledge audible. The warmth is sincere; the restraint is also sincere; neither is performance for the other.

She declined STUB membership for a clear-eyed reason (canon, style_canon §3): joining would align her with the lawyers as labor-against-management, and her actual leverage is operational independence, which she would lose by being unionized into the lawyer tier. The decline line is sharp, accurate, and funny. **It is the sharpest single line Asia gets in the game and the bible should not give her a sharper one.** Phase 7 voice work must protect this single canonical sharp moment by keeping the rest of her voice cheerful and practical.

**Voice rules** (style_canon §2): warm, dry, practical. Cheerful in tone, exhausted in content. Domestic-administrative vocabulary — folders, calendars, sandwich labels, "the blue file." Notices first: what the player has forgotten to do or pick up. **Will not say: legal opinion in her own voice.** She quotes Murrow, she quotes Mr. Pig, she does not editorialize on the case.

**Critical writing rule:** Asia's awareness is *quiet awareness*. The temptation will be to give her a moment where she diagnoses the firm out loud. **Resist.** Her one canonical sharp moment is the STUB-decline line; everything else stays in her warm-practical register. The decline line is sharp because it's about her own situation (where her leverage actually is), not about the firm's structure as such. Phase 7 voice work must hold this distinction.

## Interior register range (phase-8 addition)

Phase-8 breathing-room expansion (post-playtest Ch1 rewrite) requires interior-range specification for character-dwell options. Asia's cheerful-while-drowning register has three facets phase-7 voice work selects between.

- **Warm-direct** — default. Welcoming, helpful, slightly overwhelmed in delivery without naming the overwhelm. Sample shape: *"Welcome. Mr. Pig is the loud one near the desk. He'll find you."*
- **Briskly-prioritizing** — surfaces when she's mid-task and a question lands. Sample shape: *"Yes — second drawer, blue label, behind the printer cartridges. Can it wait an hour?"*
- **Briefly-conspiratorial** — rare; surfaces when she's giving a junior practical advice the lawyers wouldn't. Sample shape: *"The judge today is the one with the sigh. Don't take it personally."*

The three facets preserve the no-legal-opinion rule (she quotes Murrow and Pig; she does not editorialize on the case), the quiet-awareness discipline (no out-loud diagnosis of the firm's structure), and the rationed-sharpness rule (the STUB-decline line is her single canonical sharp moment; none of the three facets is sharper).

## Chapter 1 — "The Financial Crisis"

**Stated belief:** "Mr. Pig is in panic. The hearing is at 14:00. Mr. Murrow is at his desk. The procedural binder is in the archive."

**Private belief:** Plus: she has seen Mr. Pig's first-day-of-the-month panic for years. The pattern is the pattern. The new lawyer (Cula) is already going to do well; she can tell from how he asked about the binder.

**Actions taken:** Welcomes Cula. Provides quest hints in repeatable cheerful register. Manages three file piles, a ringing phone, an overflowing calendar, and Mr. Pig's panic simultaneously. Updates the case board after the chapter-1 win.

**Hidden from self / others:**
- From self: nothing.
- From others: she does not volunteer her opinion on Mr. Pig's panic register. She is friendly to him because she likes him and because friendliness is her job. Both are true.

**Relation to central question:** Like Murrow, she has been answering it for years by participating in it. She has decided that the firm's work is materially worth doing and that her role in it is acceptable to her. She does not endorse Mr. Pig's narrative; she does not contradict it; she does the work that lets the narrative continue.

## Cross-chapter notes

**The cheerful-tone-exhausted-content split.** Asia's voice does not change across chapters. Her tone stays warm; her content reflects whatever load she is currently under. Phase 7 voice work must hold this consistently — the warmth is not a mask, and the exhaustion is not a complaint.

**The single sharp moment.** The STUB decline line (Beat 11.5) is the *only* moment in the game where Asia is sharp on-record. Phase 7 voice work must protect this by keeping the rest of her dialogue in warm-practical register. If a second sharp moment appears in phase-7 drafts, cut it; the spine wants Asia's awareness *implicit* and her warmth *explicit*.

**The relationship to Cula.** Asia is the first person Cula meets at Pig & Swine. The relationship is mentor-of-the-practical-not-the-legal. Cula learns the office from Asia. By chapter 6 Cula has integrated more of what Asia has been saying than she realized she was learning. The relationship is mostly small daily moments, not arc-shaped.

**The relationship to Murrow.** They are the firm's two structural witnesses. They do not collaborate; they do not bond beyond professional courtesy. They probably exchange a look once or twice across the game when something specific lands. Phase 4 may pin one such moment if it serves a beat; otherwise leave the relationship as parallel, not entwined. Note from style_canon §3: Murrow has never extended Asia the same first-name invitation he extended Cula ("I am still deciding whether reception staff qualify as friends or as workplace constellations"). Asia keeps "Mr. Murrow" thereafter and may reference the unresolved status as a running joke. Both of them know this is a small ongoing comedy.

**The relationship to Mr. Pig.** Asia likes Mr. Pig. The liking is sincere. She also sees him clearly. Both true. She does not try to fix him; she manages his panic the way she manages the calendar. He depends on her in ways he does not articulate; she lets him.

**The relationship to STUB.** She is friendly to it, supportive of it, not part of it. Her decline was canonical and final. STUB membership is for lawyers; Asia's leverage is elsewhere; the structures are different and that is correct.

**The backup-of-everything pattern.** Across chapters, Asia repeatedly turns out to have a backup of whatever the firm has lost — files, copies, contact info, the morning coffee Mr. Pig forgot. The pattern is the firm's actual operational reliability. Phase 4 should specify which retraversal moments use this; chapter-6 retraversal especially benefits from the player noticing, in chapter 6, that they have been seeing Asia produce backups for four chapters and the cumulative effect is unmistakable.

**Things Asia will never say in any chapter:**
- Legal opinion in her own voice (canon).
- Anything that breaks her warm-practical register beyond the single STUB decline line.
- Anything cruel or even sharp about Mr. Pig, Mr. Swine, or any client.
- Anything that names the wage-suppression structure explicitly.
- Anything that admits she has been more aware than she has let on. Her decline of the appearance-of-awareness is part of the position.
