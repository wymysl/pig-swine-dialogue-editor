Two characteristic Asia rhythm patterns surface across the sequence. **Spatial-emotional pun** (state 2 *"between the files and disappointment"*; state 12 *"actively on fire"*) — concrete-emotion compound that registers awareness without naming. **Operational-absurdity aside** (state 3 *"briefly saw another dimension"*; state 11 *"expensive and irresponsible"*) — practical noun with a small absurd qualifier.
