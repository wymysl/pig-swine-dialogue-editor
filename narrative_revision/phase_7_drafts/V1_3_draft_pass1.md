## BEAT 7 — Recruit Whimsy at Café Paragraf

*[Interior. Whimsy at a corner table, eggplant velvet blazer, notebook open, a half-finished espresso at his elbow. Cula approaches.]*

**CULA:** Mr. Whimsy. Cula, Pig & Swine. We need a second voice in court tomorrow.

**WHIMSY:** Doctor. Sit. Have you anything beyond procedural vibes, or am I to be recruited on atmosphere?

**CULA:** Right to a fair hearing. Notice was served to a closed address; the objection record never entered. We file at fourteen hundred tomorrow.

**WHIMSY:** Ah. The fair-hearing doctrine, asked to do its gymnastics again. A notice that arrived nowhere, an objection that arrived twice into the void. The docket has a mood.

*[Whimsy reaches into his bag, produces a folder, slides it across the table. The top page is the Rights Memo. A coffee ring darkens the upper margin.]*

**CULA:** The Rights Memo.

**WHIMSY:** Marginalia in espresso. Half the argument is already mine.

*[Beat.]*

**WHIMSY:** I propose. The rights argument has melodic possibilities; the procedural defects are well-tuned. I will lend my voice tomorrow at fourteen hundred.

## BEAT 8 — Court readiness check (Pig & Swine office)

*[Office. Murrow at his desk, pencil down, list in front of him. Asia at reception in the V1.1 baseline staging, head down on the calendar. Cula returns with Crab and Whimsy.]*

**MURROW:** Cula.

**CULA:** Murrow. We have the memo.

**MURROW:** Procedural binder. On file.

**MURROW:** Rights memo. Now in. Coffee ring noted.

*[Mr. Pig drifts past the desk, blazer half-buttoned, papers under one arm. He stops. Speaks toward the room.]*

**MR. PIG:** The printer lease has come back as a kraken. We renew next month, presumably with bait.

*[Pig keeps walking, papers shifting under his arm. Murrow does not look up.]*

**MURROW:** Crab and Whimsy on the appearance. Confirmed.

**MURROW:** Hearing at fourteen hundred. Room three.

*[Pig drifts back the other way, slower now, hand on a coffee cup. Stops near Cula but does not look at him.]*

**MR. PIG:** When I was an apprentice, we had no readiness check. Only fear. We navigated by panic alone, and most of us reached the courtroom.

*[Pig drifts toward his own desk. Cula does not respond. Murrow continues.]*

**MURROW:** Witness order set. Crab opens, Whimsy closes. We leave at thirteen-thirty.

**CULA:** Understood.
