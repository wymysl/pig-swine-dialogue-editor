## Beat 9 — Archive Room research (NEW)

**Setup.** Cula and Murrow at the Archive Room. Murrow opens the Procedural Binder to the relevant section. The Archive Room visit is functional — they are here to apply the rule to Halina's specific facts.

**Article 135-bis § 2 KPC (fabricated text).**

> **Art. 135-bis § 2 KPC.** Service is invalid when the address used has been altered, renumbered, or otherwise made non-current to the knowledge of the serving party. A hearing held upon invalid service shall, on motion of the party not properly served, be set aside and rescheduled, provided the motion is filed within fourteen days of actual notice. The appearance of a third party at the served address shall not be considered as curing service unless that party held the express authority of the served party.

**Murrow's narration (clause-by-clause).** Three to four sentences per clause, applying the rule to Halina's facts. Phase-7 voice work specifies exact text. Recommended structure:

1. **First clause (invalid-service definition).** Murrow walks through "altered, renumbered, or otherwise made non-current." Names the 2015 renumbering. Names the landlord's knowledge (the landlord authorized the renumbering — the knowledge requirement is met).
2. **Second clause (motion + 14-day window).** Murrow walks through the actual-notice clock. Counts: Halina received the misdirected notice on [specific date]; today is [date]; Pig & Swine has [N] days remaining. The clock is tight but the firm is inside the window.
3. **Third clause (third-party non-cure).** Murrow walks through the preempted defense. The current renter of #7 is not Halina's authorized agent. The landlord's likely "but they accepted the notice" argument fails on its face.

**Character-dwell options for Cula.** Recommended option set:

- *"Have you used this clause before?"* — terse-instructional; once, several years ago; brief reference to a prior case (no detail; might recolor in Ch5 or Ch6).
- *"What do we need next?"* — dry-collegial; Murrow's checklist for court readiness; bridges to Beat 10.

**Cula's stance carrier from Beat 8 affects dwell flavor.** A sympathetic carrier produces slightly different Cula dwell lines than a technical one — same dwell content, register-aligned. Phase-7 voice work specifies.
