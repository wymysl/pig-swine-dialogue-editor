**Phase:** 5
**Inputs:** all five chapter beat sheets (`beats/chapter_1.md` through `chapter_5.md`); all eight bibles; `world.txt`; `godot/scenes/interiors/`.

**Purpose:** confirm that the funny-in-hindsight commitment is structurally supported. Every recoloring plant inventoried in the phase-4 beat sheets is consolidated here and checked for pre-inflection presence + post-inflection encounter. The map is the artifact that prevents the technique from degrading into authorial winks.
