## §A — General AI tells (cross-character; scrub from every draft)

**Mechanical scrub list (search and flag):**

- Em-dashes — count them. If more than one per paragraph, cut. AI overuses em-dashes for rhythm.
- Contrastive antithesis: "not X but Y" / "it's not just X — it's Y." **Categorically cut.** Project owner's preference forbids this pattern. **Whimsy especially vulnerable**: AI will write "It's not just procedure — it's principle" because that sounds like flourish. Cut.
- "Delve," "tapestry," "myriad," "vibrant," "navigate" (as metaphor), "showcase," "essence of," "heart of," "rich" (as adjective), "deeply," "robust," "intricate," "weave," "intersection."
- "Indeed," "moreover," "furthermore" — buttery transitions. Use only if a character would. **Whimsy may genuinely deploy archaic register** ("verily," "behold," "I propose") but should not pile on transitions.
- "Let me be clear," "I won't pretend X," "to be honest" — hedge-preambles.
- "Not only X but Y" / "X, yet Y" symmetric paired phrases.
- Rule-of-three lists for emphasis unless a specific character would. **Whimsy may use rule-of-three for theatrical effect**, sparingly.
- Generic uplifting endings: "and that, in the end, is what mattered" / "and that is enough."
- "There is something to be said for X" / "one might argue."

**Stylistic disciplines:**

- Concrete nouns over abstractions. English-equivalent legal specifics over generic abstractions. See §A.5.
- Sentences with snags. AI prose is too smooth and too symmetrical. Pig's panic specifically wants interrupted sentences; Whimsy's flourishes wrap legal content; Murrow's readiness check is short declaratives.
- Read-aloud test. If a line reads aloud as a paragraph rather than as a person speaking, rewrite.
- One canonical sincere line per character per chapter. **Sincerity is rationed.** Chapter 1 has no fully sincere line for any speaker — this includes Pig (his sincere moments accrue toward chapter 4 printer apology / chapter 6 Round 5; chapter 1 is panic register throughout); Whimsy (cynic stance is the position); Cula (observational). If a draft includes a "sincere" beat for any character in V1.3, it's over-budget — re-shape it as register-appropriate (panic / flourish / observation).

## §A.6 — Scene social context (per ai_voice_constraints §1.6)

V1.3 covers Beats 6-7, 10 of chapter 1: **café recruitment + return-to-office readiness check.** The scene moves between two social contexts that each impose their own context-awareness:

- **Café Paragraf Lane (Beats 6–7, Whimsy recruitment).** Cula approaches Whimsy at a café Whimsy uses as his rhetorical playground. Whimsy is performing a flourish to no audience; Cula is on errand from the firm. **Context-awareness for Whimsy:** he registers Cula's arrival as a new occupant of his stage, not as an interruption. The cynic stance is the register; the acknowledgment of an audience-of-one is the social texture. The flourish opens, lands, and may extend; brief acknowledgment of Cula precedes the extended performance, not as warmth but as recognition that the performance now has a witness. **Context-awareness for Cula:** professional first-day register continued — name + concrete request, no fanfare about being new, restraint in the face of theatricality. He does not match Whimsy's flourish; he does not deflate it either.
- **Pig & Swine office return (Beat 10, readiness check).** Cula returns to a familiar context — Murrow at his desk, possibly Pig audible from his corner. **Context-awareness for Murrow:** this is no longer first-meeting; the address-form invitation has landed in V1.2. Acknowledgment is now register-internal — *"Cula"* (single word; using the invited form), or non-verbal (looking up briefly, setting the pencil down) — not a repeated welcome. The ledger area should remain ordinary office furniture; do NOT flag it. **Context-awareness for Pig** (if his readiness-check intrusions land in V1.3): he is in panic register and does not necessarily acknowledge Cula's specific return — Pig's social register is talked-at, not talked-with. Lecture intrusion is the form; bypassing direct acknowledgment of Cula is in-character. **Context-awareness for Cula:** colleague-register with Murrow has been established; he uses the invited address-form, asks precise questions, does not over-narrate his progress.

**The two-test discipline (from ai_voice_constraints §1.6):** each line passes both (1) the will-not-say list AND (2) social-context awareness. A line that passes (1) but fails (2) is functional but flat — fix it with one beat of acknowledgment in the character's existing register, not by adding new content.

**Anti-pattern check:** Whimsy launching into flourish with zero acknowledgment that an audience has arrived; Murrow greeting Cula in V1.3 with the same first-meeting recognition he gave in V1.2 (the relationship has progressed); Cula over-explaining his progress to Murrow rather than asking a precise question. All are scrub-mode failures.
