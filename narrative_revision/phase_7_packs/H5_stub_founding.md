# Phase 7 Pack H5 — STUB founding scene + chapter-6 callbacks

**Goal:** author the chapter-4 Beat 11.5 STUB founding scene (post-phase-6 relocation: Crab and Whimsy at the office, Cula via phone from the courthouse, Asia declines membership at reception) plus the two chapter-6 callbacks (Crab's passing "grievance #4 still unresolved" reference; visual treatment of the faded printer sticker and the typo-ridden cork-board notice).

**Why this pack:** STUB is one of the canon's most carefully-disciplined comic devices. Style_canon §3 explicitly limits the joke to "two or three callbacks across the rest of the game; not a recurring punchline" and forbids making STUB "ever competent or effective." The pack's job is to author the founding scene + callbacks **within that discipline** — the failure mode is strengthening the union into something the canon explicitly forbids.

**Critical constraints (from style_canon §3 + bibles):**
- **The joke is the scale.** A three-person union for a half-collapsing firm. Resist scaling up.
- **Comic-deflationary, not historically grand.** The Solidarność reference (in the acronym's etymology) must not become heavy-handed.
- **Two callbacks total across chapter 6; no more.** This pack respects the budget.
- **Murrow never joins, never acknowledges in writing, files the napkin under "personal effects, see also: morale."**
- **Asia's decline is her single canonical sharp moment in the entire game** (per Asia bible). Protect the line; do not give Asia other sharp moments elsewhere in the pack.
- **Cula joins via phone** (per phase-6 Hit #11 relocation). He is at the courthouse; the founding scene is Crab + Whimsy in person.

**Estimated session length:** 1-2 hours. The founding scene is brief; the callbacks are minimal; the discipline is what takes the time.

**How to use this pack:** open a new Claude conversation. Upload this pack. Issue the §I drafting prompt at the bottom. Hand the result to a second model with §J for hostile critique.

## §A — General AI tells (cross-character; scrub from every draft)

**Mechanical scrub list (search and flag):**

- Em-dashes — count them. If more than one per paragraph, cut.
- Contrastive antithesis: "not X but Y" / "it's not just X — it's Y." **Categorically cut.** Project owner's preference forbids this pattern.
- "Delve," "tapestry," "myriad," "vibrant," "navigate" (as metaphor), "showcase," "essence of," "heart of," "rich" (as adjective), "deeply," "robust," "intricate," "weave," "intersection."
- "Indeed," "moreover," "furthermore" — buttery transitions.
- "Let me be clear," "I won't pretend X," "to be honest" — hedge-preambles.
- "Not only X but Y" / "X, yet Y" symmetric paired phrases.
- Rule-of-three lists for emphasis unless a specific character would.
- Generic uplifting endings.
- "There is something to be said for X" / "one might argue."

**Stylistic disciplines:**
- Concrete nouns over abstractions. Use English-language legal specifics over generic abstractions (see Language convention below).
- Sentences with snags. AI prose is too smooth and too symmetrical.
- Read-aloud test. If a line reads aloud as a paragraph rather than as a person speaking, rewrite.

## §A.5 — Language convention (English version)

**The English version reads smoothly for an English-speaking player with no Polish-language training.** The bibles and beat sheets use Polish-legal vocabulary internally for precision; **dialogue does not**.

**Acceptable in English dialogue:**
- Proper names of people: Borowski, Plotek, Kowalski, etc.
- Warsaw place names where flagship.
- *Solidarność* — **only in the deliberate STUB callback context**, and even then sparingly. The acronym's etymology nods to it; the dialogue should not invoke the historical movement directly. If used, no gloss needed (Solidarność is internationally recognized as the Polish union).
- PLN currency.
- Zero-to-one Polish flavor word per scene as deliberate texture, glossed inline first time.

**Translate to English** (do NOT borrow into dialogue):
- *sąd rejonowy* → "district court"
- *pełnomocnictwo* → "power of attorney"
- *wokanda* → "docket" / "cause list"
- *aplikatura* → "the apprenticeship"
- *aplikant* → "apprentice"
- *patron* → "master"
- *kancelaria* → "the firm" / "the practice"

**STUB acronym handling.** The acronym itself is canonical: **S**olidarity **T**rade **U**nion of **B**arristers. The Polish-version equivalent is **PUTKA** (*Pakt Uboczny Trzech Krytycznych Adwokatów*) but **the English version uses STUB throughout**. The typo on the cork board reads in English: *"Solidarity Trade Unon of Barrister"* (sic — missing 'i' in Union, missing 's' in Barristers). The typo is part of the joke; phase 7 may iterate the specific misspelling but the typo principle is locked.

**Critical for this pack:** the *Solidarność* reference can be invoked once if the moment lands deflationary. Recommended: a single brief mention by Whimsy in cynic register, naming the comparison and immediately undermining it ("the smallest possible echo of *Solidarność*" or similar — making the joke that the comparison is comically inadequate). Do NOT repeat. Do NOT make the comparison grand.

## §B — Per-character constraints

### Crab (opportunist; in person at the founding)

**Voice essence:** terse, observational, professionally indifferent to morale. Two-sentence rhythm: observation, then implication.

**Canonical samples:**
- *"The notice says delivered. The address says impossible. That is not service. That is postal theatre."*
- *"Follow the paper. It leads to money. Paper usually does."*

**Syntax disciplines:**
- Plain, concrete nouns. Trusts facts.
- Two-sentence rhythm.
- Notices first: the inconsistency. Always — as leverage, not as moral provocation.

**Will not say:**
- Anything reassuring.
- Anything outraged.
- Anything cruel about Mr. Pig or Mr. Swine.
- Any explicit diagnosis of the firm's wage suppression. He sees it; he doesn't crusade.

**AI tells most vulnerable to (specific to STUB founding):**
- **Outrage as motivation.** AI will write Crab as fed-up-with-injustice. Crab is not fed up; he is calculating. The STUB founding is *low-cost professional solidarity* for him — he signs the napkin because Cula and Whimsy did, and because it costs nothing. He is not radicalized.
- **Earnest commitment to the union.** Crab is the opportunist. He is committed only as far as signing a napkin. Phase 7 voice work must hold this — he does not deliver inspiring lines about workers' rights.
- **Long sentences.** Crab is terse. The STUB founding scene should have him delivering brief observations, not speeches.

**For STUB founding specifically:** Crab's role is to observe the conditions plainly ("they are doing all the actual lawyering, on no sleep, with the firm's printer literally being sabotaged by a rival, and the only payment so far this month has been a postcard from Mr. Swine") and then to participate in the founding without overcommitting. His signature is professional gesture, not commitment.

### Whimsy (cynic; in person at the founding; draws the napkin)

**Voice essence:** grandiose, theatrical, brilliant-when-aimed. Cynic dressed in flourish. Loves a flourish.

**Canonical samples:**
- *"A fair hearing is like soup. If one side gets a spoon and the other gets a fork made of bees, the court must intervene."*

**Syntax disciplines:**
- Extended metaphors with specific legal referents.
- Literary, philosophical, occasionally archaic vocabulary.
- "Verily." "Behold." "I propose."
- If forced to be technical, sneaks a metaphor in anyway.

**Will not say:**
- Anything boring.
- Anything technical without smuggling in a metaphor.
- Anything that explicitly diagnoses his own cynicism.
- Anything sincere about Mr. Pig that isn't dressed in flourish.
- Anything cruel about clients.
- Contrastive antithesis.

**AI tells most vulnerable to (specific to STUB founding):**
- **Grandiose union-founding rhetoric.** AI will write Whimsy delivering historically-resonant lines about workers' rights. Cut. **Whimsy's investment in STUB is heavier than expected** (per Whimsy bible) — he commits more than Crab does because the union is closer to his actual bitterness — but the heaviness lands as *theatrical sincerity dressed in flourish*, not as inspirational speech.
- **Solidarność-comparison made earnest.** The Solidarność reference exists in the acronym's etymology. If Whimsy invokes it in dialogue, the invocation must be deflationary — the joke is that this three-person union is the smallest possible echo of the historical movement. Do not let Whimsy make the comparison sincerely.
- **Excessive flourish density.** The founding scene is brief; one or two flourishes from Whimsy is enough. Flourish-saturation reads as AI rather than character.

**For STUB founding specifically:** Whimsy draws the napkin sketch (small drawn pig with tiny gavel, S-T-U-B beneath). His founding line is the heaviest emotional moment in his cynic register across the game — heavier than any chapter dialogue. Recommended phase 7 shape: a flourish that frames the founding as small and serious at once ("Behold, three of us against the silence" or similar). The investment surprises Cula slightly; Crab probably reads it accurately.

### Cula (player character; on the phone from the courthouse)

**Voice essence:** observational, dry, restrained. **In this scene Cula joins via phone** — brief lines only. He is at the District Court for the next hearing, called by Crab or Whimsy to confirm membership.

**Canonical samples:** voice rules per style_canon §2; sparse canonical samples.

**Syntax disciplines:**
- Precise. Short when stressed; longer when arguing law.
- Avoids slang. Never curses.

**Will not say:**
- Anything in modern internet voice.
- Anything that mocks the firm.
- Any internal narration.
- Any reference to the chapter-4 ledger glimpse from earlier in Beat 11.
- "I want to..." / "I think..." preambles.

**AI tells most vulnerable to (specific to STUB founding via phone):**
- **Cula treating the founding as significant in the moment.** Cula has just glimpsed the operating-account reserve at Beat 11 (chapter-4 inflection). The STUB founding is structurally adjacent but causally independent (per phase-6 Hit #11). **Cula's phone-call lines must NOT reference the inflection, must NOT articulate the connection between the conditions and the ledger.** He agrees to membership because the conditions are real.
- **Long phone-call dialogue.** Cula is at the courthouse for the hearing; the call is brief. Two or three short exchanges max.

**For STUB founding specifically:** Cula's role is to be invited and to agree. Recommended phase 7 shape: short, dry, slightly wry agreement. He doesn't deliver a speech; he agrees to sign the napkin tomorrow morning when he returns from the courthouse. The signature happens off-screen.

### Asia (at reception; declines membership — her single canonical sharp moment)

**Voice essence:** warm, dry, practical. Cheerful in tone, exhausted in content.

**Canonical sample (THE LINE for this pack):**
- *"Front-desk staff have negotiating power that lawyers cannot match — I have the password to the photocopier."* (style_canon §3)

**Syntax disciplines:**
- Domestic-administrative vocabulary.
- Cheerful tone holds even when content is exhausting.

**Will not say:**
- Legal opinion in her own voice.
- Anything that breaks her warm-practical register **beyond this single canonical sharp line.**
- Anything cruel.

**AI tells most vulnerable to (specific to STUB founding):**
- **Adding more sharp lines.** AI will want to give Asia additional witty observations during the founding scene. **Cut.** The decline line is her one canonical sharp moment in the entire game; everything else in the scene stays in warm-practical register. If Asia speaks more than the decline line, the additional dialogue must be brief and warm-practical (e.g., a passing question about the courier paperwork, not another quip).
- **Emotional commitment to the union.** Asia is friendly to STUB, supportive of it, not part of it. She is not invested in the union's success. Her decline is materially correct from her perspective; she does not need to add commentary about why.
- **Wisdom-of-the-receptionist register.** AI will lean into "secretary as sage." Cut. Asia is not delivering wisdom; she is declining membership for a specific operational reason.

**For STUB founding specifically:** Asia is at reception, finalizing courier paperwork from the urgent filing dispatch. She is offered membership, delivers the canonical decline line, returns to her work. The line is the moment; everything around it is warm-practical normal-Asia.
