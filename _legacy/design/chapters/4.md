# Chapter 4 — "Mr. Swine Returns"

**Sprint 3 input.** Founder returns trailing an international arbitration. Dual-perspective play introduced.

**Source-of-truth reference:** `implementation_plan.md` lines 2573–3804.

## Goal

Introduce **dual-perspective play**: Dr. Kula handles legal structure; Mr. Swine handles facts, memory, and chaotic first-hand testimony. Chapter 4 asks: what if one of the founders is the problem?

## Core loop

Swine returns → lawsuit revealed → decode absurd travel/expense evidence → investigate contract trail → play Swine flashback → dual-attorney arbitration → partial vindication + deeper secret.

## Legal spine

> A party should not be held liable for international commercial obligations unless the alleged contract, authority, performance, and billing basis are proven with sufficient clarity.

## Comedy spine

> Mr. Swine may be innocent, but he has behaved exactly like someone trying to look guilty in twelve jurisdictions.

## New mechanics this chapter

### Dual Attorney mechanic

In the arbitration, the player switches between Dr. Kula (legal structure) and Mr. Swine (factual testimony, flashback memory). UI: a turn/role indicator. State: which attorney is active.

Each round may require both:

- Kula: cite contract law, authority, formation requirements.
- Swine: provide first-hand facts that contradict the resort's narrative.

Failure mode: assigning the wrong role to a round produces weaker advocacy — Kula struggles with a memory-only point, Swine derails on legal doctrine.

### Swine Flashback mini-mechanic

A short playable flashback in Japan: Swine at the resort, signing things. Player walks Swine through the resort and chooses what to sign / not sign. Outcomes affect what evidence is available and how strong it is.

## Mini-game (new this chapter)

**Ski Slalom** — flashback sequence at the resort. Avoid commercial-document gates, collect non-binding-document gates. Win = clearer factual picture; lose = murkier factual picture (still progressing).

Self-contained module under `src/minigames/ski_slalom.js`.
